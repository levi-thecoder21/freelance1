Tour Plan

01
Day 1: Departure From Chennai To Tirupati

Morning: Depart From Chennai To Tirupati (Approximately 3-4 Hours By Car Or Train). Arrive At Tirupati And Check Into Your Hotel.

Afternoon: Visit Sri Padmavathi Ammavari Temple At Tiruchanur. Explore Kapila Theertham, a Holy Temple. Relax And Prepare For The Next Day's Pilgrimage.

02
Day 2: Tirumala Tirupati Temple Visit

Early Morning: Begin The Ascent To Tirumala. Experience The Sacred Darshan At The Sri Venkateswara Temple.

Afternoon: Have Lunch At A Local Restaurant Offering Traditional Andhra Cuisine.

Evening: Spend The Evening At Silathoranam. Return To Tirupati And Rest.

03
Day 3: Local Temples And Sightseeing

Morning: Visit The Sri Kalahasti Temple.

Afternoon: Explore The Chandragiri Fort.

Evening: Stroll Through The Tirupati Bazaar.

04
Day 4: Nature And Spiritual Retreat

Morning: Take A Short Trip To Talakona Waterfalls. Visit Sri Venkateswara National Park.

Afternoon: Enjoy Lunch At A Local Restaurant In The Area Near Talakona.

Evening: Relax And Take Part In Any Evening Rituals Or Temple Events.

05
Day 5: Return To Chennai

Morning: Visit Any Remaining Temples Or Shrines In Tirupati. Return To Chennai.
